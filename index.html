How I Work

Human insight,
AI-accelerated.

My process isn't a waterfall — it's a loop. AI enters wherever it earns its place: synthesising research, stress-testing ideas, generating variants. I own the judgment; AI compresses the time.

AI-augmented workflow Phases overlap — AI helps me move fluidly between them rather than waiting for one to finish.
01
🔍

Discover

User interviews, competitive analysis, analytics deep-dives. I listen hard before I draw a single pixel — because the right solution rarely comes from the first guess.

AI here: Interview synthesis Sentiment analysis Competitive intel Pattern recognition
02
💡

Define

Turn findings into sharp problem statements, user flows, and design principles. AI stress-tests assumptions and surfaces blind spots before a single frame is drawn.

AI here: Hypothesis refinement Persona generation Journey map synthesis
03
✏️

Design

Lo-fi to hi-fi, tested with real users. AI accelerates variant generation and copy so I can spend creative energy on the decisions that actually require taste.

AI here: Component variants Copy generation A11y audits Design critique
04
🚀

Deliver

Developer-ready specs, a living design system, and a designer who stays in the loop through build. AI auto-generates documentation so nothing gets lost in handoff.

AI here: Spec documentation Handoff notes QA flagging
